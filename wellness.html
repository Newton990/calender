Cycle Academy 🎓 Learn your biology

Menstrual Phase (Days 1-5) 🩸

Hormone levels are at their lowest as your body sheds the uterine lining.

▼

This is the start of your cycle. Your energy is naturally lower, and your body is focused on rest and renewal. It's a time for reflection and inner focus.

Key Biological Events:
  • Estrogen and Progesterone drop to their lowest levels.
  • The hypothalamus signals the pituitary gland to start the process again.
Follicular Phase (Days 6-13) 🌱

Hormones start to climb, bringing fresh energy and mental clarity.

▼

Your brain is extra sharp now. It's the perfect phase to start new projects, socialize, and tackle complex problems. You'll likely feel more optimistic and creative.

Key Biological Events:
  • FSH (Follicle Stimulating Hormone) rises to mature eggs.
  • Estrogen begins to thicken the uterine lining.
Ovulation Phase (Day 14-16) ✨

Peak fertility and peak confidence! You're glowing inside and out.

▼

This is a short window with big impact. You might feel more attractive, communicative, and physically strong. Your libido is often highest during these few days.

Key Biological Events:
  • LH (Luteinizing Hormone) surges, triggering the release of the egg.
  • Estrogen reaches its highest point.
Luteal Phase (Days 17-28) 🌙

The 'winding down' phase. Turning inward and prioritizing stability.

▼

The first half is calm, but as you approach your period, you may feel more sensitive. It's a great time for detail-oriented tasks and finishing projects rather than starting them.

Key Biological Events:
  • Progesterone rises to prepare the uterus for potential pregnancy.
  • If no pregnancy occurs, hormone levels drop, triggering menstruation.

Daily Wellness Tips

  • Start your morning with a glass of warm lemon water.
  • Take 5 minutes for deep breathing every afternoon.
  • Limit blue light exposure 1 hour before sleep.
  • Move your body for at least 20 minutes today.
🌸

PMS Support

  • Apply a warm compress for cramp relief.
  • Ginger or Chamomile tea helps reduce bloating.
  • Magnesium-rich foods can help with mood swings.
  • Gentle stretching/Yoga relieves lower back tension.
🍎

Nutrition Suggestions

  • Leafy greens for iron during your period.
  • Avocados and healthy fats for hormone balance.
  • Probiotics (Yogurt/Kefir) for digestive health.
  • Dark chocolate (70%+) for a mood boost!
🧘‍♀️

Self-Care Reminders

  • It's okay to say 'no' and rest when you need it.
  • Write down three things you are grateful for today.
  • Your worth is not defined by your productivity.
  • Treat yourself to a relaxing bath or a quiet walk.
